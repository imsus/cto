# Delegation - Sources & Further Reading

## manager.dev Articles

- [Why Engineering Managers Give Up](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/why-team-leaders-give-up) - Team Rep pattern, Epic Ownership, Knowledge Ownership, and the root cause of the EM bottleneck
- [What I wish somebody told me during my first year as a manager](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/a-letter-to-the-first-time-manager) - Team Rep, go-to person trap, and first-year delegation mistakes
- [Most micromanagers are blind to being seen as one](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/most-micromanagers-are-blind-to-being) - Task-Relevant Maturity and delegation principles
- [Give your engineers a kingdom](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/give-your-engineers-a-kingdom) - kingdom ownership framework and four ownership types with decision authority
- [Who does what and how to support them](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/who-does-what-and-how-to-support) - three-layer assignment framework: efficiency, advancement, durability

## Books

- *Turn the Ship Around* (L. David Marquet) - intent-based leadership; "I intend to..." shifts ownership to the team
- *The Dichotomy of Leadership* (Jocko Willink) - micromanagement vs. under-management symptoms as a diagnostic
