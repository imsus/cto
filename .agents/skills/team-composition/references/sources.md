# Team Composition - Sources & Further Reading

## manager.dev Articles

- [Build your engineering team like a dungeon party](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/build-your-engineering-team-like-a-dungeon-party) - Warrior, Tank, Healer, Wizard, Rogue archetypes for diagnosing team skill gaps

## Books

- *An Elegant Puzzle* (Will Larson) - minimum team size around four engineers; never build a team around a single person

## External Articles

- [How to operate (barrels and ammunition)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6fQHLK1aIBs) - Keith Rabois; barrels vs. ammunition; velocity is limited by barrels, not headcount
- [Commandos, Infantry, and Police](https://cdixon.org/2010/02/18/the-three-phases-of-business-growth) - Robert X. Cringely / Chris Dixon; three company phases requiring different engineer types
