# Management Transitions - Sources & Further Reading

## manager.dev Articles

- [The Apprentice, The New Boss, The Successor and The Pioneer](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/the-apprentice-the-new-boss-the) - four transition types and the risks/actions for each
- [Being an Engineering Manager at IKEA businesses](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/being-an-em-at-ikea-businesses) - acquisition integration; build trust before changing systems or processes
- [Don't become an Engineering Manager](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/dont-become-an-engineering-manager) - counseling engineers on the management track and structural headwinds of the role

## Books

- *The Making of a Manager* (Julie Zhuo) - the four types of management transitions: Apprentice, Successor, Pioneer, New Boss
- *The Art of Leadership* (Michael Lopp / Rands) - Blue Tape List: tag what feels wrong, wait 30 days, then address

## External Articles

- [Fixing broken teams - don't blame the previous leader](https://mgmt.beehiiv.com/p/fix-your-broken-team-8-turnaround-tips-for-sustained-success) - turnaround process: name baggage, reset target, revise behaviors
- [The 6 Mistakes You're Going to Make as a New Manager](https://terriblesoftware.org/2024/12/04/the-6-mistakes-youre-going-to-make-as-a-new-manager/) - dopamine shift from shipping to enabling; delegation reluctance
- [How Engineering Management is Changing in 2024](https://refactoring.fm/p/how-engineering-management-is-changing) - post-2022 shifts in the EM role
