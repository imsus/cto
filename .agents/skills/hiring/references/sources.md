# Hiring - Sources & Further Reading

## manager.dev Articles

- [Hiring ONLY seniors is the worst policy in the software industry](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/hiring-only-seniors-is-worst-policy) - five reasons to hire juniors, river-not-lake team model, internship advantage, and pay based on skills
- [The #1 new engineering managers mistake](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/the-1-new-engineering-managers-mistake) - concrete 30/60/90 goals and the "would I hire today?" checkpoint
- [You are firing people too late](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/you-are-firing-people-too-late) - 90-day evaluation window, relief test, and sunk-cost trap
- [Managing people you don't like](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/managing-people-you-dont-like) - beer test bias and how similarity bias damages hiring decisions

## Books

- *Peopleware* (DeMarco & Lister) - see the work before you hire; work samples reveal what interviews miss
