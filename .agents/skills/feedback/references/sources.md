# Feedback - Sources & Further Reading

## manager.dev Articles

- [Being an engineering manager at Amazon](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/being-an-engineering-manager-at-amazon) - precision, no weasel words, and specificity in both critical and positive feedback
- [6 assumptions engineering managers make (and engineers hate)](https://newsletter.manager.dev/p/6-assumptions-engineering-managers) - public praise is not universal; ask how someone wants to be recognized

## External Articles

- [Stop serving the compliment sandwich](https://adamgrant.substack.com/p/stop-serving-the-compliment-sandwich) - Adam Grant; four-step alternative to the compliment sandwich
- [How to boost remote speed feedback](https://www.bitsandbrushes.news/p/how-to-boost-remote-speed-feedback) - Bits & Brushes; speed feedback activity for building a feedback habit

## Books

- *Radical Candor* (Kim Scott) - Care Personally, Challenge Directly; honest feedback as an act of care
- *The Making of a Manager* (Julie Zhuo) - opening a critical feedback conversation; discomfort as a signal the conversation is real; silence as data
